Tomahawk Volume 2

As Nanabush makes his way across the land during one of the coldest winters in recent times, he hears many stories of people disappearing into the frigid woodlands in the dead of night. He knows the work of evil spirits when he sees it, and the medicine man looks to put a stop to them. All he needs is a good heart, a sharp mind, and his trusty pair of tomahawks.
